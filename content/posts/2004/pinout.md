Standard Ethernet (568B)
------------------------
1 WhiteOrange 	<->	WhiteOrange
2 Orange	<->	Orange
3 WhiteGreen	<->	WhiteGreen
4 Blue		<->	Blue
5 WhiteBlue	<->	WhiteBlue
6 Green		<->	Green
7 WhiteBrown	<->	WhiteBrown
8 Brown		<->	Brown

-----------------------------------
1 WhiteOrange 	<->	WhiteGreen
2 Orange	<->	Green

4 Blue		<->	Blue
5 WhiteBlue	<->	WhiteBlue
6 Green		<->	Orange
7 WhiteBrown	<->	WhiteBrown
8 Brown		<->	Brown

------------------------

8 Brown		<->	Brown

-----------------------------------
1 WhiteGreen	<->	WhiteOrange
